      SOCIAL MEDIA DO’S AND DON’TS CHECKLIST


Do’s
• Think before you post 
• Use strong passwords for your accounts 
• Keep your personal information private 
• Be respectful and polite in comments 
• Verify information before sharing 
• Use privacy settings to control who sees your content 
• Share positive and meaningful content 
• Log out from shared devices 


 
Don’ts
• Don’t share passwords or OTPs 
• Don’t click on suspicious links 
• Don’t post personal details (address, phone number, etc.) 
• Don’t spread fake news or rumors 
• Don’t bully or harass others online 
• Don’t accept requests from unknown people 
• Don’t overshare your daily activities 
• Don’t use inappropriate language or content
